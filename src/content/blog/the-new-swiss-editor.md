title: "The new Swiss-inspired editor"
date: 2026-01-01
label: "Design"
description: "We redesigned the entire editor interface around Swiss modernist principles — clean grids, strong typography, and maximum clarity."
---

We rebuilt the Pasta editor from scratch. The new design is rooted in Swiss modernism: strict grids, Bebas Neue for display type, generous whitespace, and a two-colour palette (black and red).

Every control has one job. Every label is terse. The goal was an interface that gets out of the way of the document.
