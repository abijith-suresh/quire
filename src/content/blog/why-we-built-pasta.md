Most online PDF tools work by uploading your file to a server, processing it, then sending it back. That means a stranger's computer holds your documents — even briefly.

Pasta does the same work inside your browser using [pdf-lib](https://pdf-lib.js.org/). Your files never leave your device. No account, no upload, no trace.
